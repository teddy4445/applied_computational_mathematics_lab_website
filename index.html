Who We Are

We are a multidisciplinary research group dedicated to developing innovative computational methods and mathematical models that address complex problems across science, engineering, and industry. ACML is a scientific research lab at the Information Systems Department at the University of Haifa (Israel) together with the Computing Department at Jonkoping University (Sweden).

Mathematical Modeling

Developing sophisticated mathematical frameworks to represent complex real-world phenomena.

Simulation Design

Creating efficient simulations and computational methods to capture complex real-world dynamics and solve complex optimization problems.

Applied Solutions

Translating theoretical advances into practical applications across multiple domains and industries.
