Authorship Deal Risk Checker

Estimate how fragile your proposed author arrangement is before submission.

This tool flags likely tension points around order, credit, and late-stage renegotiation. It is a conflict-risk estimator based on transparent heuristics inspired by game-theoretic authorship-dispute dynamics, not a judge of correct authorship.

What this checker surfaces

Use it before submission to see where an authorship deal may become brittle, who may feel under-credited, and which decisions are safest to settle now.

  • Contribution-order mismatch for visible byline positions.
  • Equal-contribution labels that may not resolve underlying tension.
  • Late-stage pressure, utility asymmetry, and advisor-student power context.
